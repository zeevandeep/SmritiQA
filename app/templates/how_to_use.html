How to use Smriti

Your AI-powered journaling companion

Interactive App Tour

New to Smriti? Take the interactive tour to learn about all features and how to get the most out of your journaling experience.

Getting Started with Voice Journaling

1 Start Recording: Go to the Journal tab and tap the microphone button to begin recording your thoughts.
2 Speak Naturally: Share your thoughts, feelings, and experiences as you would with a trusted friend.
3 Submit: When finished, tap stop and submit your journal entry for AI analysis.

Viewing Your Journal Entries

Access all your past journal entries in the Entries tab. You can:

  • View your complete journaling history
  • Expand entries to read the full transcript
  • See when each entry was created

Understanding Your Reflections

Smriti analyzes your journal entries to generate personalized insights:

  • Pattern Recognition: Identifies emotional and cognitive patterns in your thoughts
  • Connection Mapping: Links related thoughts across different journal sessions
  • Personalized Insights: Provides empathetic reflections to help you understand yourself better

Generate New Reflections

Use the Generate tab to manually create new reflections when you want fresh insights from your journaling history.

Privacy & Security

Your journal entries are private and secure. Smriti processes your thoughts with care and confidentiality to provide meaningful insights while protecting your personal information.
